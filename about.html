Our Journey

Signal is a globally trusted oral care brand under Unilever, dedicated to promoting healthy smiles for families worldwide. Since its introduction in 1961, Signal has been at the forefront of oral hygiene innovation, combining science and care to deliver toothpaste that not only protects teeth but also builds confidence through a bright, healthy smile.

Over the decades, Signal has continuously evolved its product range with advanced fluoride technology, cavity protection, whitening formulas, and natural ingredients to meet the changing needs of customers. Today, Signal stands as a symbol of oral health education and accessibility across Asia, Africa, and Europe.

Our Core Values

  • Promoting oral health education across communities.
  • Developing safe, effective, and affordable oral care products.
  • Innovating with science-backed formulas for better protection.
  • Encouraging families to adopt healthier dental habits.
  • Supporting eco-friendly and sustainable practices.
